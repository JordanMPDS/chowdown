tags: german, soup

ingredients:
- 1 egg
- 1 cup milk
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt

directions:
- Beat egg in medium bowl
- Stir in milk
- Stir in flour, baking powder, and salt results in a very soft dough.
- Place 1/2 tsp sized balls in simmering soup
